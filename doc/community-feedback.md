# Community Feedback

## Feedback #1

- Can't see password when typing it in — would be nice to have a show/hide toggle.
- On the submission form, I can click on courses to filter but clicking on difficulty doesn't do anything. My instinct told me it was a button. 
- Flashcards are showing up for terms that don't have an approved definition yet. Kind of pointless to study those.
- There's no shuffle option for flashcards.
- Is the extra credit cumulative across all submissions or just the approved one? Genuinely cannot tell. Please clarify this somewhere visible.

---

## Feedback #2

- The map/graph view is cool but when I navigate around, I always get lost and there's no way to snap back to the center. Maybe a "recenter" button?
- Add image viewing on the instructor side. Right now you can submit image-based definitions but I can't actually view them properly.
- Notifications build up fast and I can't clear them. Even just a "mark as seen" click would be enough.
- Maybe a way to filter or sort terms by week? It gets overwhelming when everything is listed together.

---

## Feedback #3

- A report/flag function for submissions would be really helpful for moderation. Not everything inappropriate needs a full manual review process.
- Please make it clearer that extra credit is per course on the platform itself, maybe a tooltip or FAQ.
- Would be nice to have a direct messaging feature within StudyViser so students don't have to go outside the platform to reach instructor.

## Feedback #4

- Overall the platform is really cool and I can tell a lot of work went into it!
- Small thing: it would be nice if the password field had a visibility toggle.

- A possible report button for submissions would make the class feel safer and more moderated.

## Feedback #5
 
- The study guide is really cool but the known/still learning progress doesn't save when you leave the page. Every time I come back I have to re-sort myself, which defeats the whole purpose of marking cards in the first place.
- On top of that, there's no way to filter down to just the cards I marked "still learning." I have to keep clicking through ones I already know just to get to the ones I need to practice. A "study weak cards only" mode would be really helpful, especially close to an exam.
